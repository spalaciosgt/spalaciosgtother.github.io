Target Audience

The target audience for this website and the information it will provide, essentially it is for people who wish to mountaineering or climbing, specifically:

  • Enthusiastic citizens who want to do amateur mountaineering.
  • Mountaineering athletes who want to know the weather conditions for an expedition.
  • Anyone who is interested in knowing the weather conditions in mountains, volcanoes, peaks and others in Guatemala.

Personas

  1. Andrés is an amateur mountaineer, but he belongs to the Federation of Mountaineering. With a training group, he organizes one or two expeditions a month to train.
  2. Ethan is a family man who loves outdoor activities with his family. He and his family go climbing mountains or volcanoes at least one time every three months, it is a family tradition.

Scenarios

  1. Andrés, to ensure the safety and success of his excursions or training expeditions, use mountainweather.org to know what weather conditions will be in the place they will climb, be it a mountain, volcano, peak, or the geographical feature whatever.
  2. Andrés loves mountainweather.org because apart from the temperature, humidity and climate levels, you can find specific data depending on the type of geographical feature, for example if it is a volcano, you can see if there are alerts of suspected eruption, seismic fluctuations, etc.
  3. Ethan loves mountainweather.org because he can get all the data he needs to make sure the weather will be perfect for him and his family and have a perfect experience. He can see very accurate weather predictions.
  4. Another thing Ethan loves about mountainweather.org is that he can see the opinion or ratings of predictions made rated by other people, which makes him feel more confident in the decisions he will make when traveling and make your outside activities perfect.
