Member Benefits

Take advantage of our membership benefits

Networking

In the constant evolution of the market that is constantly changing and transforming more quickly, networking takes a primary role in the strength and growth that every company is looking for. For this reason, the Chamber of Commerce encourages networking and the use of contacts, providing spaces in which entrepreneurs can interact, meet, offer or seek services that facilitate or make their company more productive.

Business Directory

The Guatemalan Chamber of Commerce, always at the forefront, has developed the Business Directory for more than three decades, with the aim of promoting integration and exchange between entrepreneurs. The Business Directory is the preferred tool used by executives and businessmen looking for contacts to buy and sell products, establish business relationships and form strategic alliances; that you can now also find it in its digital version.

Training

Chamber of Commerce of Guatemala is always in search of current issues that can contribute positively to the constant training of its partners' staff in all its areas. For this reason, the training department always seeks to generate content of interest and innovation to provide educational growth support to companies and their collaborators.

Bussines Advice

Members of the Chamber of Commerce have access to advice in various forms, legal, tax and labor, thus covering each of the branches in which the business environment can have an impact in one way or another. So that the Member can feel the tranquility of being supported by an institution that looks after their interests.
